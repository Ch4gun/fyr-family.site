About us

FYR is more than just a group of clans - we are a structured, supportive, and family-friendly community focused on teamwork and long-term growth.

It's all about teamwork and having fun

Our core values

Fun - The main reason we play. Without it, nothing else works.

Growth - Learning and improving keeps us motivated.

Community - Real people, teamwork, and mutual support define us.

Respect - Tolerance, understanding, and fair treatment are essential.

Sustainability - We plan ahead and adapt so our clan stays strong long-term.

Clan requirements

Every clan member should follow our core values, support other players, and avoid drama and toxic behavior.

1. Weekly chest points

  • Collect minimum chest points every week
  • Points can be earned by exploring crypts or killing epics

2. Ancients Tournament event

  • Vaults are mandatory
  • Full participation required
  • Minimum points based on G/S/M level

3. Trials of Olympus event

  • Collect personal rewards every day
  • Lava Fountains and Explore Tartaros Crypts on days 3 and 4 are mandatory
  • Buy all Hermes chests

4. Dark Omens event

  • Attack Acronomancer to collect all personal rewards
  • Follow leadership instructions for summons and essence usage

5. Ragnarok event

  • Buy all Jörmungandr chests
